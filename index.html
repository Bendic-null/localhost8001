Our Story

How we started and where we're going

Founded in 2020, TechVia began with a simple mission: to bring the most innovative tech gadgets directly to consumers without the traditional retail markup. What started as a small operation in a garage has grown into a thriving e-commerce platform serving thousands of customers worldwide.

We believe technology should be accessible to everyone. That's why we carefully curate every gadget in our catalog, ensuring it meets our high standards for quality, innovation, and value. From cutting-edge smart devices to everyday tech essentials, we're committed to offering products that enhance your digital lifestyle.

Our Journey

From startup to innovation leader

2020

The Beginning

TechVia was founded in a small garage with a vision to revolutionize tech e-commerce. Started with just 10 carefully curated gadgets.

2021

Scaling Up

Expanded our catalog to 50+ products and reached our first 1,000 customers. Launched same-day delivery in major cities.

2022

Going Global

Extended shipping to 15 countries and opened our first international warehouse. Introduced AI-powered product recommendations.

2023

Innovation Hub

Launched our tech testing lab and exclusive early-access program. Partnered with leading gadget manufacturers worldwide.

2024

Community Growth

Reached 5,000+ satisfied customers across 25 countries. Introduced TechVia Premium membership and 24/7 expert support.

2025

The Future is Now

Continuing to push boundaries with cutting-edge gadgets, sustainable tech initiatives, and unparalleled customer experience.
